Ecodream Design eCommerce

Ecodream Design is an eCommerce website specialized in selling made in Italy, hand-crafted sustainable bags and related accessories.

Description

  • Purpose: capire i problemi di usabilità e accessibilità del website Ecodream e ripensare il design di 5 pagine del sito in base ai problemi individuati durante il processo di ricerca UX.

  • My role: UX/UI Designer

  • Collaboration: just me and tutor feedbacks

  • Timeline: no due date

  • Tool: Figma

Process

Personas

I identified three female user personas across different age groups, reflecting the target demographics. Each persona represented varying levels of tech proficiency and distinct purchasing behaviors.

(Here I'll put just one of them)

User Journey Map

For each user persona, I created a user journey map with three different scenarios. Based on the identified pain points, I outlined the opportunities that would form the foundation for my eCommerce redesign work.

Accessibility

I conducted the same work of identifying user personas and creating user journey maps for three individuals with disabilities, in order to address accessibility issues within the eCommerce platform

Wireframes

At the wireframing stage, I first designed Lo-FI wireframes on paper. Once I identified the appropriate design, I created Hi-Fi wireframes in Figma. This process was far from static. As i began working on the site's UI, I noticed issue that weren't apparent in the static wireframes which were limited to grayscale. This made the wireframing stage iterative.

Design System

Final Design

The final resul is a responsive design: it adapts seamlessly to both desktop and mobile devices.

Final thoughts

What I Learned

This was my first UX/UI design project, making it crucial for applying everything I had learned. Completing the project entirely on my own was immensely valuable. I faced several challenges along the way, which forced me to solve problems indipendently by conducting exstensive reserch, learning from those with more experience, and adopting industry best practices.

Each phase of the project was reviewed by a senior professional in the field, which was critical in helping me understand not only what I was doing wrong but also what I was doing correctly. This allowed me to adjust my approach and refine my way of working.

Clearly, this is just the beginning, but this project has been incredibly motivating and has helped me realize what I want to do and what truly excites me.
